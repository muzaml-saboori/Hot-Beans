About Hot Beans

Our Story

Hot Beans was founded in 2025 by Christofer Surtherlannd in a small office in Haringey. What started as a one-person freelance operation has grown into a team of 35+ talented designers, developers, and strategists.

We started with one simple belief: great web development should be available to businesses of all sizes, not just the big players with massive budgets. That belief still drives everything we do today.

Over the years we have delivered over 120 projects for clients ranging from local small businesses to national brands, always with the same commitment to quality, honesty, and results.
